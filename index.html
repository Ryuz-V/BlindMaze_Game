Game Instructions

Memorizing Phase

You have 10 seconds to memorize the maze layout. Walls are shown during this phase.

Moving Phase

You have 20 seconds to reach the finish point. Walls become invisible during this phase.

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move your player through the maze.

Hint

You have 1 hint per stage. It will reveal the walls for 1 second during the moving phase.

Lives

You start with 5 lives. Hitting a wall or running out of time costs 1 life.
